Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how GoldBullion ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

GoldBullion is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

The App does not require you to create an account or sign in.

2. Data Usage

The App fetches publicly available gold and silver price data and currency exchange rates from third-party APIs solely to display live rates within the App. This data is:

  • Fetched in real time and displayed on your device
  • Not stored on any server controlled by us
  • Not linked to any personal information

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

a) Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including but not limited to:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device and app usage data
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

Google AdMob's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your device's Settings → Google → Ads and enabling "Opt out of Ads Personalization."

b) Price Data APIs

The App retrieves live gold and silver prices from Gold-API and exchange rates from ExchangeRate-API. These services may log standard request metadata (such as IP address) as part of their own operations. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

4. Data Storage

GoldBullion does not store any user data locally or remotely. All price data displayed in the App is fetched in real time and is not persisted after you close the App.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information handled by the App. All network requests are made over secure HTTPS connections. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.
